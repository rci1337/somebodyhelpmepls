Windows 10 vs. ATLAS

These tests are conducted on Atlas v0.5.2 & stock Windows 10 21H1.

Processes

Atlas disables and strips lots of processes which run in the background on stock Windows. Processes are not too important, but you can compare your results to ours on the left, it is mind blowing.

145 -> 35~

Ram Usage

All the bloated applications and processes that run in the background add up to effect a substantial amount on your total ram usage. We have gone ahead and removed the stuff you do not need.

2.3GB -> 900MB~

Process Latency

Latency is a sticky topic, but let us tell you, having a lower process latency is going to be better than having more. Atlas wins again, like the champ operating system it is.

3.09 -> 2.55
